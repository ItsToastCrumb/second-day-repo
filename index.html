Chiitan is a national treasure!

Origin

The mascot of Chiitan is based off an actual Asian small-clawed otter of the same name. Chiitan was created by the same designer of Shinjo-kun, the official otter mascot of Susaki in Japan. Since the real otter was named as Susaki's honorary tourism ambassador, Chiitan declared itself the unofficial "tourist ambassador."

Antics

Chiitan gained great popularity on Youtube and social media with their crazy antics. Their videos and posts would range from learning a new skill to outrageous slapstick humor. Some of Chiitan's trademark antics include beating up an inflatable punching bag with a baseball bat, running at high speeds on a treadmill, and crashing while zooming down a slide or street.

Controversy

Some of the citizens of Susaki were unhappy with Chiitan's "reckless" stunts and complained that it represented the city poorly. Even though it was discovered that the citizens mistook Chiitan for their official mascot, Shinjo-kun, the city ended up cutting ties with Chiitan and asked that they cease activities. Chiitan's Twitter accounts were then suspended although their Instagram account remained active. John Oliver even addressed this whole situation on his HBO program and entered into a humorous beef with Chiitan.
